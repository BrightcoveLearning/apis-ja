    Brightcove University Training Courses

    This topic lists the Brightcove University training courses that are currently available.

    Video Cloud

    Video Cloud Courses
    Media Management with Brightcove Video Cloud https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/681885
    In this course, you will learn how to manage your content to create the best possible experience for your viewers using the Upload and Media Modules in Video Cloud.
    Creating and Scheduling Custom Analytics Reports https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/681885
    Custom analytics reports allow you to define exactly what you see in Analytics reports and to run them either once or on a daily, weekly, or monthly schedule. Reports are available in CSV or XLSX format, and you can get notifications of new reports emailed to yourself and others. This course shows you how to create and manage custom reports.
    Administering Video Cloud https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/512605
    Administering Video Cloud provides an introduction to many of the administrative functions you can perform in the Admin section of Studio. It will show you how to add additional users, create custom video metadata fields, share videos between multiple Video Cloud account, manage ingest profiles that control how your videos are processed when you upload them, and how to create client credentials that can be used to authenticate API requests if you have developers who will be using Brightcove's APIs.
    Introduction to Video Cloud Analytics https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/511393
    Viewing the Introduction to Video Cloud Analytics training course is a great way to become familiar with using Video Cloud Analytics. This course was designed for publishers who are just getting started with Video Cloud.
    Introduction to Video Cloud https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/499241
    Introduction to Video Cloud is an absolute must for those who upload, configure, and maintain online media. Learn valuable best practices that will help you save time and resources. This course is a packed session designed to meet the needs of your content producers, project managers, and editors.
    Introduction to Brightcove Player https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/841750
    Welcome to Introduction to Brightcove Player.

    For this course, we recommend that you take the Media Management with Brightcove Video Cloud course first.

    In this course you will learn:

    1. How to create a new player in Video Cloud
    2. How to configure your player:
      • Edit player properties and customize the player appearance
      • Add plugins
      • Learn more about the player configuration page
    3. How to preview and publish content
      • Preview and publish changes in the player
      • Publish a video in the media module with the new player

    Brightcove Gallery

    Brightcove Gallery
    Introduction to Gallery https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/504770
    This introductory course will provide you with an overview of Brightcove Gallery including the types of video experiences that you can create with it, and its settings.

    Prior to starting this course, we recommend completing our Introduction to Video Cloud course which is available in the catalog.

    After completing this course, you may be interested in our additional training on the different types of experiences that you can build. You can find Building In-Page Experiences Using Gallery and Building Portal Experiences Using Gallery in our catalog as well!

    Building Portal Experiences using Gallery https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/504795
    In this course, we will walk you through the process of creating, customizing, and publishing a Gallery Portal experience. You will learn about the Gallery settings, how to create a new experience, add videos to a site, customize the site properties, and how to preview and publish a Portal experience.

    We recommend that you have a working knowledge of Video Cloud before starting this course. In the catalog, you can find our Introduction to Video Cloud course that will give you a good foundation prior to starting this. We'd also recommend going through our short Introduction to Gallery course if you would like an overview of what's possible with Gallery.

    Building In-Page Experiences Using Gallery https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/504717
    This course was designed for publishers who are new to Gallery. We recommend that you have a working knowledge of Video Cloud before taking this course. Take the Introduction to Video Cloud course before getting started.

    Brightcove Audience

    Brightcove Audience
    Introduction to Brightcove Audience https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/513099
    The Audience module is used to track video engagement data for videos that are viewed inside of an Audience-enabled Brightcove Player. Viewing data is stored in Video Cloud and can optionally be synchronized to popular marketing automation platforms such as Eloqua, Marketo, HubSpot, and Salesforce.

    Introduction to Brightcove Audience will show you around the Audience module and explain how you can use the tools that Audience provides.

    Brightcove Social

    Brightcove Social
    Introduction to Brightcove Social https://university.brightcove.com/catalog/courses/662145
    Brightcove Social allows you to easily publish videos in your Video Cloud account out to popular social media platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, and Twitter.

    Introduction to Brightcove Social will give you an understanding of the different parts of the Social module, and help you get started with sharing your videos out to social media platforms. You will also be able to track the history of sharing, set up auto-syncing in some cases, so that you don't have to share each video manually, and view some analytics on how your video is performing on the various social platforms.
